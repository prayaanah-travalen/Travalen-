Cancellation Refund:

Cancellations made within 48 hours of the booking date will incur a 100% cancellation charge.

Cancellations between 2 to 7 days will result in a 50% cancellation charge.

Cancellations between 8 to 14 days will have a 25% cancellation charge.

No cancellation charges apply for bookings canceled 15 days or more in advance.

Postponement Refund:

Users are entitled to one-time postponement with every booking.

Postponements must be requested a minimum of 10 days before the booking date.

No additional charges for booking postponements.

The postponement window is a maximum of 60 days from the original booking date.

Priority will be given to the original hotel during postponement, subject to availability. If unavailable, alternative properties will be provided, and users must confirm the new bookings.

TRAVALEN.IN acts as a facilitator and is not liable for issues beyond its control, such as delays, improper services, or incidents caused by service providers.

TRAVALEN.IN is not responsible for personal injury, loss, or damage caused by co-travelers or service providers.

Refunds are not applicable for issues beyond TRAVALEN.IN's control, including delays in hotel check-in due to technical snags or reasons not attributable to TRAVALEN.IN.

Refund Claim Process:

Users must address any discrepancies in hotel information directly with the hotel.

Refund claims for eligible cancellations or issues must be communicated to TRAVALEN.IN's designated contact within a reasonable timeframe.

Refund Disbursement:

Refunds, when applicable, will be processed in accordance with the cancellation or postponement policies.

TRAVALEN.IN reserves the right to deduct any transaction fees or charges incurred during the refund process.

Refund Mode and Refund Time

Refund mode will be same payment method used to booking.

Refund will be processed in 1 to 3 business days.

Policy Updates:

TRAVALEN.IN may update this refund policy to reflect changes in business practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant updates.

User Rights:

Users have the right to seek a refund in accordance with the outlined policies.

Requests for refunds should be directed to TRAVALEN.IN's designated contact.

By using TRAVALEN.IN, users agree to the terms outlined in this refund policy. It is advised to regularly review the policy for any updates.
